Product designer with a systems mindset — from discovery and research through shipped UI and design-system contribution.

Bio

Over the past several years I've worked on B2B and operations-heavy products where users are under time pressure and errors are costly. I start with the workflow, not the mockup: who acts, what decision they're making, and what success looks like in numbers.

I collaborate closely with PMs and engineers — writing specs that hold up in build, facilitating critiques that focus on outcomes, and maintaining component libraries so teams move faster without visual drift.
